Our Services

Pet Grooming

Give your furry friend the pampering they deserve! Our full grooming service includes a relaxing bath, a stylish trim, nail care, and more. We’ll leave your pet looking fabulous and feeling fresh, ready to strut their stuff.

Pet Sitting

Enjoy peace of mind while you're away with our reliable pet sitting services. Whether it's feeding, playing, or simply keeping your pet company, we ensure they’re cared for with love and attention, just like you would.

Pet Walking

Let your dog stretch their legs and enjoy the outdoors with our daily dog walking service. We make sure your pup stays active, healthy, and happy with tailored walks designed to meet their energy level and personality.

Overnight Care

Your pet’s comfort and safety are our top priorities during overnight stays. Whether it’s a cozy bed, lots of cuddles, or their favorite treats, we create a home-away-from-home experience for your beloved pet.

About Us

At Whiskers, we believe pets are family. That’s why we’re dedicated to providing the highest level of care for your furry companions. Whether it’s grooming, sitting, walking, or overnight care, we’re here to make life easier for both you and your pet.

Our team is passionate about animals and brings experience, love, and attention to every tail wag and purr. We take the time to get to know each pet, understanding their unique personalities and needs. Your pet’s happiness and safety are our top priorities.

When you choose Whiskers, you’re not just getting a service—you’re joining a community of pet lovers who want the best for their four-legged friends. Let us help keep your pets happy, healthy, and loved while you enjoy peace of mind.
